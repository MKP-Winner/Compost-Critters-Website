Our Mission

üåü Critters Purpose

  • Eco-friendly plant and compost kits in cute, animal-themed boxes
  • Designed to be fun, hands-on, and engaging for young children
  • Teaches kids how to grow plants and compost their own food
  • Encourages understanding of sustainability and eco-friendly habits
  • Aims to promote environmental awareness and positive daily practices

‚ö†Ô∏è Problems Today

  • Wastefulness: Every day, people unknowingly throw their leftovers in the bin. We aim to address food insecurity.
  • Accessibility: Understanding and combating food insecurity can be challenging for newer generations.
  • Education: People don't know how to use the food they normally waste in a meaningful way.

üí° The Solution

  • Repurpose: We will begin to repurpose wasted food from local communities like schools and farms by making compost
  • Accessibility: We provide an easy way for anyone to support fighting food insecurity.
  • The Next Generation: Our compost-making kits not only provide an educational tool for the next generation to teach them how to utilize the food they waste.

üë• Our Audience

  • Our target audience is children, as we hope to deepen their connection with the environment.
  • Through these cute kits, our aim is to provide kids exposure to sustainability at a young age to foster interest, familiarity, and positive habits as they grow up.
  • We hope to share this project with:
    • Education Centers
    • Local Gardens
    • Elementary Schools

‚ôªÔ∏è Why Compost

  • Our Compost is a more sustainable option that tackles food waste compared to other options.
  • It is made from:
    • "Unsellable" farm food
    • "Unwanted" school food
    • "Unutilized" food
  • We transform food waste into a resource, returning nutrients to the earth to grow new food instead of letting them end up in a landfill.
